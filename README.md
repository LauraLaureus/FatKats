# FatKats
   Two kitties, Luffy and Pastelitio, are hungry in the streets of Japan. Take the food, hit your oponent or die. 
   
   Local Multiplayer:
   
   Player 1: 
   
   A: left
   
   D: right
   
   W: jump
   
   S: dash your opponent
   
   Player 2:
   
   left arrow: left
   
   right arrow: right
   
   up arrow: jump
   
   down arrow: dash your opponent
   
   Granada JAM - entry
